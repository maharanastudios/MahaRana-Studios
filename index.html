Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services and process.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A mobile app typically takes 3-6 months, websites 4-12 weeks, and IoT solutions 2-4 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during the discovery phase.

What's your development process?

We follow agile methodology: Discovery ‚Üí Planning ‚Üí Design ‚Üí Development ‚Üí Testing ‚Üí Deployment ‚Üí Support. Regular communication and weekly updates keep you informed every step of the way.

Do you provide post-launch support?

Yes, absolutely. We offer maintenance packages including bug fixes, updates, performance optimization, and feature enhancements. Support can be on-demand or through fixed retainers.

What technologies do you work with?

We're technology-agnostic and choose the best tools for each project. Common selections: React, Next.js, Angular for web; Flutter, React Native, Swift for mobile; Node.js, Python for backends; and ESP-IDF for IoT.

How much does a project cost?

Costs depend on project scope, complexity, and timeline. We offer flexible engagement models: fixed-price, time & material, and retainer-based. Get a free consultation and detailed quote.

Can you integrate with existing systems?

Yes, we specialize in integrations with existing platforms, APIs, and databases. Whether it's payment gateways, CRMs, or legacy systems, we handle complex integrations smoothly.
